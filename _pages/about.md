Welcome to my website!

I am an independent researcher from Brazil.
My current work focuses on developing efficient neural network architectures for general problem-solving over natural language.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the [Federal University of Juiz de Fora](https://www2.ufjf.br/).
For my undergraduate thesis, I investigated the computational power of different neural network architectures using theoretical computer science.
During my degree, I also did research on brain-computer interfaces and EEG signal classification using deep learning techniques.

My current research interests include the following:

- Deep Learning
- Reinforcement Learning
- Artificial Intelligence
- Program Synthesis
- Language Modeling

I also write a [blog](/blog) on artificial intelligence and neural networks, so make sure to check it out!
